Welcome to My Blog

November 24, 2025

Welcome to my technical blog! I'm excited to launch this platform where I'll be sharing my journey, experiences, and insights from working as a Principal Engineer.

Why This Blog?

Throughout my career in software engineering, I've learned that sharing knowledge is one of the most valuable things we can do as technologists. This blog serves multiple purposes:

Knowledge Sharing. I want to contribute to the broader engineering community by sharing the lessons I've learned, both successes and failures.

Documentation. Writing about technical concepts helps me solidify my own understanding and creates a reference for future me (and hopefully others).

Connection. I hope to connect with other engineers, engage in meaningful discussions, and learn from the community.

What to Expect

In this blog, you'll find a variety of content including technical deep-dives, system design discussions, thoughts on leadership and culture, and career growth advice.

A Bit About Me

As a Principal Engineer, I've had the privilege of working on diverse projects, from building high-scale distributed systems to leading technical initiatives across organizations. I'm passionate about creating elegant solutions to complex problems and empowering teams to do their best work.

You might know me as ShaiKKO in various tech communities. I'm always eager to learn, always questioning assumptions, and always looking for better ways to build software.

Let's Connect

I'd love to hear from you! Whether you have questions, suggestions for topics you'd like me to cover, or just want to share your own experiences, please don't hesitate to reach out.

Thank you for stopping by, and I hope you find value in the content I'll be sharing here. Stay tuned for more!
