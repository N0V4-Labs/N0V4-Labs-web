About Me

I'm Colin MacRitchie, also known as ShaiKKO. I build systems that have to work at scale—engines, tools, infrastructure.

Most of that work right now is on PHALANX. The articles here are where I write about the decisions that come out of it: the tradeoffs, the surprises, and the things that looked good on paper but fell apart under load.

If you're interested in low-level design, mechanical sympathy, or game engine internals, you'll probably find something useful here.
